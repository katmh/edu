# Physical Computing – Week 1 – Rube Goldberg Machine

Since this class is at 9am, I don’t wake up early enough to make breakfast. By yanking a cord on this Rube Goldberg machine (still from bed), a variety of breakfast ingredients could move in the kitchen to ultimately depress the handle on my toaster & begin toasting a bagel.
